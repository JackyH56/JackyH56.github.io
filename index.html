- Project Showcase -

Converse and Cook

This web app will be used by the leaders and volunteers of Converse and Cook to make grocery shopping for their cooking events easier. Volunteers and leaders will be able to add recipes to the app through a user-friendly interface. The software will store the recipe and make it accessible for other users as well as the general public to see. Anyone will be able to see recipes and change the units of its ingredients or scale the serving size, which facilitates the grocery shopping process. After shopping is done, volunteers can upload their receipt in order to be reimbursed by the organization.
